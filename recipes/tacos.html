Tacos!

Check out these delicious healthy tacos for your next dinner.

Ingredients have been provided below.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Tomates
  • Avocados
  • salt
  • Your choice of beef, chicken, fish
  • Cheese
  • jalapenos
  • cilantro
  • Chile serrano

Steps

  1. First make your salsas, cook your tomates in boiling water along with your chiles serranos
  2. Place your boiled tomates and chiles serranos in your blender, add salt and ajo and onion, blend them until in liquid form.
  3. Now your salsa is ready pour it onto a bowl and place it aside.
  4. Now cook your choice of meat (chicken, beef, fish)
  5. Now chop up your veggies, tomates, cilantro
  6. extract your avocados, place all the good stuff of the avocado in a bowl, grab a spoon or a plastic cup and mash them up until in a mushy form
  7. Place guacamole in a bowl, place veggies into separate bowls
  8. get your tortillas heat them up, place your choice of meet on them, add veggies, add guacamole, add cheese, add salsa
  9. Thank god for the food, and Eat!
