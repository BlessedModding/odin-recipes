Pizza

A deliciously cooked olive healthy pizza!, it has olives, tomato, cheese, whole grains. MMMM very healthy!.

I have provided the ingredients for this delicious pizza below, go ahead and check them out.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Whole grain
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Prepare your flour
  2. Pan out your flour to pizza specification
  3. Add the raw ingredients to your flattened circular flour
  4. Place whole raw pizza inside oven bake at 370 degrees F for about 25-30 minutes
  5. last step, Eat your pizza!
