Pinto Beans

Great source of protein beans, healthy for the whole body, and tasty to eat!

I have provided the instructions on how to cook these beans, the instructions are specific for the exact beans shown in the picture, they are pinto beans

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ajo
  • salt
  • onion
  • pinto beans

Steps

  1. Clean your beans out, look for rocks in the beans; remove them
  2. Place cleaned out beans, water, salt, onion, ajo in pot
  3. Place heat to medium-high for a moderate cook, cooking time is 1:30 hr-2:30hr
